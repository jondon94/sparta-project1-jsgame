WELCOME TO HELL

You have died, but even hell cannot stop you. Get past each level and reach the surface to rejoin the living.

  • Don't touch the fire, it's hot.

Controls

  • Left arrow = left
  • Right arrow = right
  • Up arrow = jump
  • Down arrow = duck
  • To climb a wall press right/left and up.
